Submission Guidelines
Abstracts must be 300–500 words in English or German. Authors must indicate their preferred track and presentation format. All submissions undergo double-blind peer review by the Scientific Committee.
Publication
Selected papers will be published in the Conference Proceedings (ISBN-registered). Extended versions may be submitted to partner journals including Zeitschrift für Sozialwissenschaften and the Journal of Digital Governance.
